MAKE YOUR OWN CHESSPRESSO MARTINI

You’ve heard about it somewhere on the internet but you still have no clue whatsoever about it. Well, look no more. Here’s the original recipe for the CHESSPRESSO MARTINI.

First of all, be sure to have these ingredients:

  • Kahlúa
  • Ice cubes
  • Vodka
  • Espresso

We assume you have a shaker where to put everything. So yes, that’s what you can do next. Grab a shaker and fill it up with all the ingredients mentioned above.

The parts vary but the standard is 40ml of vodka, 20ml of Kahlúa and 20ml of Espresso. Don’t forget the ice cubes.

Now seal the shaker and start shaking baby. The trick to a perfect CHESSPRESSO MARTINI is to shake it exactly 32 times, one for each piece of a chess set.

There you go. Time to impress your partner with a check-mate cocktail. Enjoy!
